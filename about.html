Policies

Internet & Computer Use Policy

Guidelines for accessing the library's computers and internet resources.

  • Computer Access: Available to all members with a library card.
  • Session Limits: Computer sessions are limited to 1 hour during peak times.
  • Appropriate Use: Users must follow the library's code of conduct and avoid accessing inappropriate content.
  • Wi-Fi Access: Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the library premises.

Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users.

  • Data Collection: We collect only the information necessary to provide library services.
  • Confidentiality: Personal information is kept confidential and is not shared without consent.
  • Online Privacy: Our website uses encryption to protect users' data.
  • Access to Records: Users can view their own borrowing history and personal information.
